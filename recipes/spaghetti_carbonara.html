Spaghetti Carbonara

This spaghetti carbonara recipe is a classic Italian dish that's both simple and delicious.

With its creamy sauce, crispy bacon, and cheesy flavor, it's sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Bacon or pancetta
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the bacon or pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the pan with the bacon.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture, ensuring the eggs cook evenly and create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.
